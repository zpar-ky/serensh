cheeseburger mac & roasted broccoli

calories: 1,160

carbs 75g, fat 65g, protein 61g, (27%, 52%, 21%)



ingredients

  • 3lbs ground beef/turkey (beef 80/20 turkey 85/15)
  • 1 lb penne pasta (one container)
  • 200g shredded carrots
  • 1 white onion
  • 90g tomato paste
  • 450g whole milk
  • 575g low sodium vegetable broth (more if needed)
  • 1 tbsp chili powder (from a farmers market in new mexico, if possible)
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 2 cups shredded cheese (one bag, cheddar is ok, parm probably works)
  • 1 bunch of spinach (or two, whatever)
  • 2 bags of broccoli florets
  • olive oil to coat broccoli


steps

  • preheat oven to 450
  • in a tall saucepot cook the turkey/beef til it's mostly done. cut up onion while that's going and prepare the broccoli on a sheet pan by coating in oil and s&p
  • add [onions, carrots] and cook for 5 mins. throw broccoli in the oven whenever it's preheated
  • add [tomato paste, broth, milk, flour, chili powder, garlic powder] and bring to a boil
  • when it hits a boil, turn to low and add pasta. cook to your desired doneness. add more liquid if it really needs it, but be careful not to add too much
  • take out broccoli before its burnt unless you like some char then cook it some more
  • once pasta is done, turn off heat and add [cheese, spinach]. incorporate and season with s&p to taste
