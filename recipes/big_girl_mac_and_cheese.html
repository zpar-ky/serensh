big girl mac & cheese

calories: 1,150 (5 servings)

carbs 91g, fat 57g, protein 70g, (32%, 44%, 24%)



this recipe takes a bit more effort, requiring a blender to make the cheese sauce, and another pan for the vegetables. it also smells like garbage when im reheating it in the microwave, i dunno


ingredients

  • 3 lbs ground turkey (85/15)
  • 2-4 carrots
  • 1 pound rigatoni pasta (bag)
  • 1-2 red bell peppers
  • 16 oz 4% cottage cheese (full fat, one container)
  • 1 large white onion
  • 480g whole milk
  • ~480g reserved pasta water
  • 545g riced cauliflower (one bag of frozen)
  • 8 oz sharp cheddar (one block)


steps

  • boil some water in a large stock pot and throw pasta in when ready
  • start cooking ground turkey in large pan. season with [cumin, garlic powder, onion powder] and whatever else you feel like
  • chop [onion, carrots, peppers] and add to another pan with some olive oil
  • dice the cheddar block into half-inch-ish cubes
  • once the vegetables are softened, toss them into a blender along with the [cottage cheese, milk, pasta water, cauliflower] - it takes me two rounds to process it all
  • toss everything into the pasta pot and stir to combine. add s&p and whatever else.
