lunch overnight oats

calories: 1,120

carbs 75g, fat 83g, protein 30g, (26%, 64%, 10%)


Mix into a 14oz mason jar.


ingredients

  • 37g oats (old fashioned, whole, etc)
  • 8g black chia seeds
  • 10g coconut flakes (unsweetened, unsulfured)
  • 7g flaxseed meal
  • 5g cocoa powder (reg/dutch works, and i just use a tbsp to scoop)
  • 100g whole milk
  • 100g full fat coconut milk
  • 50g full fat greek yogurt
  • 15g avocado oil (best quality you can buy)
  • 13g honey (good local honey)
  • 66g peanut butter (extra crunchy)
  • frozen blueberries to fill (low-bush variety is good because they're smaller)


steps

add [oats, chia, flaxseed, coconut flakes, cocoa powder] and give a quick stir to distribute.

add [milk, coconut milk, yogurt, avocado oil, honey] and stir to mix

add pb and stir til it's mostly distributed. make sure to scrape sides and bottom.

top with blueberries.


fridge and take out in the morning so it's room-temp enough by lunch.
